Mars Exploration Program

Pioneering the exploration and potential colonization of Mars through advanced rovers, orbiters, and future crewed missions.

Impact: 5 rovers deployed on Mars surface
Key Initiatives:
  • Autonomous Mars rover missions
  • Atmospheric research and sample collection
  • Habitat development for future colonization
  • In-situ resource utilization studies
  • Mars-Earth communication systems
